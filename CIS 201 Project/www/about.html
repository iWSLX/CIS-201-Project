About Us
Verdant is a clothing brand built by students, for everyone.
Verdant is a website where we not only sell different kinds of clothing, but also share the story behind each piece. A well-made garment is worth a thousand words. Our mission is to offer sustainable, minimal fashion designed for real life — comfortable, durable, and honestly priced.
